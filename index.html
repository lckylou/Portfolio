My Story

While in school for finance, my entrepreneurship professor suggested that we create a website as a sort of resume of the future. So as any good student, I did some research, watched a lot of Youtube videos and started to put together my website. I soon realized that my site needed a few more things than just my resume on it. After all, who wants to look at just a static, one page, online version of my resume. So I started researching how to make logos, graphics, animations, and even videos. Soon I was so deep into the graphic design world that I started to re-think my major and decided to transfer marketing. It was after that change I realized I was on the right path. Creating a unique, simplistic, and easy to read website, logos, and designs was my passion and future.
